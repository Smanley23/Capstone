SharkShell is a secure "all-in-one" dashboard designed to keep your most important stuff—like bank accounts, private files, and business data—behind a safe; technically sound shield.

This is a digital vault that doesn't just lock the door, but also sets a "decoy" trap for anyone trying to break in.

Key features:
Secure Access: A single login gives you access to multiple "nodes" like your Private Bank, Cloud Storage, and Photo Vault.

Honeypot: If a hacker tries to guess your password or clicks on things they shouldn't, the app triggers a "Fake Hack" screen. It makes the intruder think they're winning while it actually locks them out and logs their activity.

Double Verification: For high-stakes tasks like banking, the app sends a 6-digit code to make sure it's really you.

How to use it
Install: You'll need Python and two small plugins (customtkinter and passlib).

Login: Enter your credentials. If you mess up too many times, the "Silent Alarm" will trigger.

Manage: Use the tabs to check your security logs, manage your bank account, or adjust your settings.

Exit: Always use the Red Logout button to ensure your session is fully encrypted and closed.

Why use it?
SharkShell protects your real data by hiding it behind a wall of decoys, keeping you one step ahead of anyone snooping around.
